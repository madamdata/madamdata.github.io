This Humid House - Sayang-sayang

Sayang-sayang is an installation by botanical design firm This Humid House, installed at the Esplanade Concourse. Working with filmmaker Ivan Tan and cinematographer Winson Ho, I helped produce a short video documenting the installation.



Aperture

A work of devised theatre by Matthew Armstead, Rebecca Muszynski, Victoria Lange, Parker Matthews and Ada Adhiyatma. The apocalypse dramatized through the human body. Please contact me for a private link to the documentation.



Moor Mother - Circuit City

An Afrofuturist play / poem about the cyberpunk housing crisis and the transcendence of Black time travel. A collaboration with Moor Mother, Mental Jewelry, and Irreversible Entaglements.
